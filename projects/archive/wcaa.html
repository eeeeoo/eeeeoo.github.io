Wellesley College Alumnae Association, offers life-long resources to connect students with the college and alumnae around the world.

As the communications and graphic designer, I worked on boosting social media presence, analyzing WCAA's connection with alumnae through research and data visualizations, print communications, and created marketing materials for events.

Information Graphics

These are highlights from email surveys conducted with Wellesley College alumnae between April 6 and April 20, 2016. The Wellesley College Association sent an email to approximately 28,000 alumnae.

I compiled survey results from Survey Monkey by creating data visualizations using the class colors (red,green,yellow,purple).


2016 Survey Results from Wellesley Alumnae




Social Media

I started a social media calendar to support external relations, development, technical and program areas and all other communications efforts. In order grow social audience, I also coordinated a campaign and initiated Hootsuite to meet development goals.


Archive Photos

Influenced by the #TBT campaign, I used archive photos from Wellesley College Archives to increase social media readership by 200%.


Hootsuite

After competitive analysis on social media management platforms, I utilized Hootsuite to schedule posts and Google Analytics to create reports on progress.
